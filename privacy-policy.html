Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Stretch Time LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website stretchtimesp.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, and phone number when you book a session or contact us directly.
  • Booking information — appointment details, service preferences, and payment information collected through our booking platform, PocketSuite.
  • Usage data — information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent on the site, referring URLs, and device/browser information. This data is collected through Google Analytics.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects data such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This data is anonymized and aggregated — we cannot identify you personally from this data.

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information on how Google uses your data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and confirm your appointments
  • To communicate with you about your sessions
  • To process payments securely through PocketSuite
  • To improve our website and services using aggregated analytics data
  • To send appointment reminders or follow-up communications (only if you have opted in)

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enable Google Analytics functionality. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • PocketSuite — our booking and payment platform. View their privacy policy at pocketsuite.io.
  • Google Analytics — website analytics. View Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share your data with service providers necessary to operate our business (such as PocketSuite for booking and payment processing), and only to the extent required to provide those services.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Payment information is handled entirely by PocketSuite and is never stored on our website.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at stretchtimesp@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
