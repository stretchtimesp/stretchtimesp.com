The Practitioner

Performance-grade care, at your door.

The Method

Not all stretching is equal.

Assisted stretching is a hands-on practice where a trained specialist guides your body through deeper, more targeted movement than you could achieve alone.

Using techniques like PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation), Roberto works with your nervous system — not against it. The result: faster mobility gains, relief from chronic tension, and lasting improvements self-stretching simply can't deliver.

Assisted Stretching
✦Practitioner-guided, hands-on technique
✦Reaches deeper muscle groups
✦PNF and advanced modalities
✦Real-time feedback and adjustments
✦Corrects imbalances and compensations
Self Stretching
–Limited by your own flexibility
–Often general, not targeted
–Basic static techniques only
–No real-time correction
–May reinforce existing imbalances
